Mummy Dogs

Description

A yummy mummy treat that will fill you up as you spend a spooky night in your crypt. Super easy and great for parites or night alone.

Ingredients

  • 1 tube of crescent rolls
  • 8 hot dogs
  • Mustard, for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper; set aside.
  3. Roll out crescent rolls into one sheet, placing 2 traingles together by pinching the seams to make a square.
  4. Using a pizza cutter or knife, cut each rectable into 6 strips.
  5. Wrap 3 strips around each hot dog, leaving room for the "eyes."
  6. Place into oven and bake until golden brown, ~20 minutes.
  7. Let cool for 5 minutes before adding dots of mustard for the "eyes."
  8. Serve immediately.
