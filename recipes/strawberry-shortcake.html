Strawberry Shortcake

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 6c fresh strawberries
  • 1/3c white sugar
  • 2 1/4c all-purpose flour
  • 1/4c white sugar
  • 1tb baking powder
  • 1/4tsp salt
  • 1/2c cold unsalted butter, cut into 1/2in pieces
  • 1 lightly beaten large egg
  • 3/4c half-and-half
  • 2tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2c whipped heavy cream
  • 1/4c powdered sugar

Directions:

  1. Step 1: Gather the ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch cake pan with butter and lightly dust with flour.
  3. Step 3: Slice the strawberries and toss them with 1/3 cup of white sugar. Set aside.
  4. Step 4: Combine flour, 1/4 cup of white sugar, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl; cut in butter with a pastry blender or two knives until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Make a well in the center of mixture; add beaten egg, half-and-half, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Stir mixture until just combined and no dry spots remain, being careful not to overmix.
  5. Step 5: Spoon batter evenly into prepared cake pan.
  6. Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the cake comes out clean and top is golden brown, about 18 minutes. Let cool partially in cake pan, about 10 minutes. Flip cake onto a clean work surface.
  7. Step 7: Meanwhile, beat heavy cream, powdered sugar, and remaining 1 teaspoon vanilla with an electric mixer on medium-high speed until soft peaks form, about 3 minutes. Set aside.
  8. Step 8: Cut partially cooled cake in half horizontally, making 2 layers; set top layer aside. Drain reserved strawberries in a colander placed over a small bowl, reserving any juice. Brush strawberry juice evenly over top of bottom cake layer. Evenly arrange ½ of the drained strawberries over bottom layer of cake.
  9. Step 9: Spread ½ of whipped cream evenly over strawberries; place top cake layer over whipped cream. Top with remaining strawberries and whipped cream.
  10. Step 10: Serve and enjoy!
